Description

Update FLStock and FLIndex objects with stock assessment results

Details

If both objects have the same number of iterations, the FLStock slots will be replaced by the a4aFit slots, in case of 1 iter, or a4aFitSA slots, in case of n iters. If one of the objects has 1 iter and the other n, the method will simulate using the fit results from the a4aFitSA object, to update the slots of the FLStock object.
