collapse seasons

Usage

collapseSeasons(stock)

Arguments

stock
a FLStock object

Value

a FLStock object

Description

Method to collapse seasons of FLStock objects. M and catch-at-age are summed while mean weights at age, maturity at age and mortalities before spawning are averaged.
